Is my cat a cow?

I was really worried when I saw my cat eating grass, because it seemed so strange and out of character. At first, I even joked to myself that maybe he secretly wanted to be a cow. It just didn’t make sense to me why he would suddenly start munching on plants. But after doing a bit of research, I discovered that it’s actually a very common behavior in cats. Apparently, many cats eat grass for digestion or to help with hairballs. Even though it’s normal, I still find it kind of funny and a little weird that my cat enjoys snacking on grass every now and then, but I am still worried about his health.
